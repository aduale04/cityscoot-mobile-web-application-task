With a top speed of 25 km/h and a range of 40 km, the Blast electric scooters are the perfect urban mobility solution for getting around a modern city like London. Scooters are lightweight and can easily be folded and carried. They can also be switched to traditional foot mode, if the battery is empty.
Blast scooters features include:
  • GPS/GPRS devices and anti-theft systems.
  • Fairlight anti-slip brakes.
  • High capacity 60V batteries with a typical four-hour charge life.
  • Height adjustable handlebars.
